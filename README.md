# Name of my project:keen-keeper 

# A short description: 
KeenKeeper is a personal relationship management tool designed to help users maintain and nurture their most meaningful bonds. In a fast-paced world, it’s easy to lose touch; KeenKeeper ensures you stay connected with your friends and family through scheduled check-ins and interaction tracking.


##  Technologies Used
- **Framework:** Next.js (App Router)
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS & DaisyUI
- **Icons:** Lucide-React & React Icons
- **State Management:** React Context API
- **Charts:** Recharts (for Analytics)


---

##  Key Features

### 1. Intelligent Friendship Tracking
Stay on top of your social life with a dynamic 4-column grid display of all your connections. Each friend card shows real-time status updates (**On-track**, **Almost Due**, or **Overdue**) based on your last contact date, making it easy to see who needs your attention.

### 2.  Quick Check-In System
Log interactions with a single click! From the friend detail page, you can instantly record **Calls**, **Texts**, or **Video Calls**. This automatically updates your friendship timeline and triggers a toast notification, providing a seamless user experience.

### 3.  Friendship Analytics & Timeline
Visualize your social habits with the **Analytics Page**, featuring interactive Pie Charts that break down your interaction types. Additionally, the **Timeline Page** keeps a historical log of all check-ins, allowing you to filter by interaction type to review your history.
